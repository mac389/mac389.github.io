Danish Language

I have been interested in learning Danish ever since I traveled to Iceland in 2018. (I traveled on WOW airlines a year before it imploded). I remember taking an elevator up to the top of the Alþingi with a few tourist whose language I couldn’t place. I chalked it up to my lack of familiarity with Germanic languages.

Over the next year I started working on Icelandic. While reading Independent People by Halldór Laxness, I realized that the language I overheard could be Danish. So, I bifurcated. (NB: Include Mermaid Block)
