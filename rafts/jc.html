Background

Saul Bellow wrote there is simply too much to think about. A scientist must think and, guided by that thought, do. Acquiring knowledge is a skill, as is distilling knowledge into wisdo. This notebook is my attempt to record my reading of the scientific literature and make explicit the conceptual links I form between articles, to engage with the literature of my areas of interest just as I have tried to do with the classics.
